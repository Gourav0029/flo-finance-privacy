# Privacy Policy for Flo Finance

Last updated: 03/07/2026

Flo Finance ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal finance and budgeting
application. This policy explains how the app handles your information. We have
designed Flo Finance to be privacy-first: your financial data stays on your
device.

## Information We Store

All financial information you enter into Flo Finance — including transactions,
budgets, savings goals, challenges, and related notes — is stored **locally on
your device**. This data is encrypted on your device and is **not** transmitted
to us or stored on any external server. We do not have access to it, and we
cannot see, collect, or recover it.

## AI Coach Feature (Google Gemini)

Flo Finance includes an optional AI spending coach powered by Google's Gemini
API. When you use this feature, the financial context needed to answer your
request is sent to Google's Gemini service to generate a response. This data is
processed by Google in accordance with Google's privacy policy
(https://policies.google.com/privacy). The AI Coach is the only feature that
transmits any data off your device. If you do not use the AI Coach, no financial
data leaves your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal or financial data on our
servers. We do not use advertising. We do not sell or share your data with third
parties. We do not track your location. The app does not require you to create an
account.

## Device Permissions

Flo Finance requests only the permissions required for its features to function.
It does not access your contacts, messages, call logs, or location.

## Data Security

Financial data is encrypted and stored locally on your device. Because your data
never leaves your device (except as described in the AI Coach section), you
remain in control of it at all times. You can permanently delete all app data at
any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

Flo Finance is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not
knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the
"Last updated" date above.
